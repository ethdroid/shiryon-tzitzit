The problem

Every wash, the same knots.

Tzitzit strings tangle, twist, and fray in the machine. Untangling them wastes your time and shortens the life of a garment you use every day. Shiryon threads all four tassels into one secure clip, so they come out of the wash exactly as they went in.

  • Holds all strings through washer and dryer
  • Snaps on in seconds with no tools and no tying
  • Durable, reusable, one clip per corner

How it works

Three seconds per corner

Thread

Gather the strings of one tassel and lay them into the open clip.

Snap

Close the clip until it clicks. The strings are held straight and separate.

Wash & dry

Machine wash, machine dry. Unclip to strings that look brand new.

Why preorder?

Your order builds the mold.

Shiryon Tzitzit is a small, founder-run project. Injection molding is what makes the clip affordable and durable, but the mold itself is the single biggest upfront cost. Every preorder goes directly toward that tooling, and every preorder customer is first in line when the run ships.

The name comes from the Hebrew word shiryon, which means armor. That's the whole idea: a small piece of armor for a mitzvah you carry with you every day.

Ethan Cohen

Founder, Shiryon Tzitzit
